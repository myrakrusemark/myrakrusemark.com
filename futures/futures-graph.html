The Long View Reveals Something Important

American democracy as we know it is historically recent. For most of the nation's history, the majority of adults couldn't vote. The "golden age" of high democracy scores (1965-2015) was an anomaly built on hard-won expansions of suffrage. What we're experiencing now isn't a fall from some eternal norm—it's a potential reversal of gains that required sustained struggle to achieve.

About the V-Dem Liberal Democracy Index

The Varieties of Democracy (V-Dem) Liberal Democracy Index measures protection of individual rights, rule of law, legislative constraints on executive power, and free and fair elections. Data extends back to 1789.

Scale: 0.80+ = Full democracy | 0.60-0.80 = Flawed democracy | 0.40-0.60 = Competitive authoritarianism | Below 0.40 = Authoritarian

The Divergence Point: 2025

We stand at a branching moment. The historical trajectory is fixed—the choices ahead are not. Each path represents different assumptions about human agency, institutional resilience, and collective action.
