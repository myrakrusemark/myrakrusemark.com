Honorable Mentions

Visual Auto Attendant

2011 • Masergy

Telephone extension management tool in a multi-column layout. This fully responsive management system made it simple to search for and manage individual business phone lines. The tool allowed public members to call telephones directly from the company website, and allowed businesses to implement real telephone contact via their website.

NRUF (National Research University Fund Eligibility)

2012 • University of North Texas

Bar-chart visualization comparing values held by UNT with other universities who received funding. Won Texas Association for Institutional Research award for best visualization of data in 2012.

Graduate Project

2009 • Webster University

Novel approach to watching custom video through a touch screen kiosk. Users slide a white dot across a spectrum of topics, causing video transcript snippets to float up as previews. An algorithm stitches together short video clips from interviews with students and professionals including Jason Silva based on user selections.
