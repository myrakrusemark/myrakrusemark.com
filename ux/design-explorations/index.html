Note: The designs shown below have been recreated using AI generation to preserve the design concepts while respecting intellectual property considerations. These recreations represent the original design approaches and solutions developed during these projects.

Digital Delivery

Exploration of digital document delivery patterns for healthcare members. This design focuses on making important documents easily accessible while providing clear status indicators and download options.

ID Card

Conceptual design for displaying member ID cards in a digital format. The exploration emphasizes clear presentation of essential information while maintaining the familiar card metaphor that members expect.

Progressive Display

Investigation into progressive disclosure patterns for complex healthcare data. This approach gradually reveals information as users need it, reducing cognitive load while maintaining access to detailed content.

Recent Claims

Design concept for presenting claims information in a scannable, accessible format. The exploration balances the need for detailed claim data with the importance of quick comprehension and status updates.
