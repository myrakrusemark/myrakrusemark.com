Frequently Asked Questions

How long will my drawing take?

Most sketches are ready within 3–5 days. Detailed color pieces may take up to two weeks. You’ll get an estimated delivery date when we confirm your order.

Can I request changes?

Absolutely! One round of small revisions is included in every package. Major changes (for example, switching characters) may require an extra fee.

Do you draw copyrighted characters?

Sorry, I can only create original characters or fan art clearly meant for personal use. I can’t sell art of licensed characters for commercial purposes.

What file formats will I receive?

You’ll receive a high‑resolution PNG or JPG. If you need something different (PDF, TIFF, etc.) just let me know in advance.
